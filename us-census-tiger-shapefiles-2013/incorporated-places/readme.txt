Census Shapefiles (2013)
Provided by Steven Romalewski @SR_spatial http://spatialityblog.com/

The shapefiles were originally obtained from the Census Bureau's TIGER 2013 download site in geodatabase format, then converted to shapefile format. Each file is a nationwide coverage of the following geographies (in no particular order):

Census Designated Places
Incorporated places
Elementary school districts
Secondary school districts
Unified school districts
State legislative districts: lower house
State legislative districts: upper house
113th Congress district boundaries
